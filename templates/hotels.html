Plan

Day 1:Arrival in Kuakata
  • Arrive in Kuakata, settle in at Hotel Grand Safa Inn.
  • Explore the nearby Kuakata Beach and enjoy the sunset view from the beach.
  • Budget for meals: Try local eateries and street food options.
Day 2: Trip to Fatrar Char
  • Wake up early and have breakfast at the hotel.
  • Arrange transportation to Fatrar Char. You might need to hire a local boat or take a group tour.
  • Spend the day exploring Fatrar Char's natural beauty, walking along the shores, and enjoying the tranquility.
  • Capture some memorable photos of the picturesque landscapes.
  • Return to Kuakata in the late afternoon.
  • Evening relaxation at the hotel or a leisurely stroll around Kuakata.
Day 3: Local Sightseeing and Departure
  • Have breakfast and check out of the hotel.
  • Visit the Kuakata Buddhist Temple and enjoy the view of the Bay of Bengal.
  • If time allows, you can also visit the Lebur Char and Gangamati Reserved Forest.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Depending on your departure time, you can spend some more time at the beach or exploring the local markets.
  • Depart from Kuakata with wonderful memories.
Important Tips
  • Be sure to check the local weather forecast before your trip and pack accordingly.
  • Since your budget is limited, consider trying local street food and eateries for cost-effective meals.
  • Always carry drinking water, sunscreen, and insect repellent during your outdoor activities.
